June 5 World Environment Day

World Environment Day, celebrated every year on June 5th, is a global event aimed at raising awareness and encouraging action to protect our environment.
             It serves as a reminder of our responsibility to preserve nature and promote sustainable living. 
             People around the world participate in activities like tree planting, clean-up drives, and environmental education programs.
              Each year focuses on a specific theme to address pressing environmental issues such as climate change, plastic pollution, or biodiversity loss.
               This day inspires individuals, communities, and governments to work together for a healthier planet.
